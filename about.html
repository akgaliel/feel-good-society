The Gap We Saw

Why We Started

Feel Good Society was born from a simple observation: so many people living with disabilities or mental health challenges were staying home not because they wanted to, but because the social world didn't make them feel like they belong.

We believed that was worth changing, so we did. Now we create inclusive social events, designed from the ground up to feel safe, fun, and genuinely welcoming for everyone who walks through the door — any day of the week.

Lamyah Galiel
Founder, Feel Good Society

I started Feel Good Society after years working alongside people living with disability and mental health challenges, seeing just how isolating life can feel when the world isn't designed with you in mind. Feel Good Society is built from both professional experience and a deeply held belief that everyone deserves moments where they feel safe, accepted, and completely themselves.
