FAQ

Common Questions

Who are the dinners for?
[Our dinners are for people living with disabilities, mental health challenges, and anyone who wants to meet good people in a relaxed, welcoming environment. You don't need a diagnosis or a referral — just a desire to have a good night out.]
Do I have to come alone?
[Not at all — you're welcome to bring a friend, carer, or support worker. Support workers attend free of charge. Just let us know when you book so we can sort seating.]
What if I get overwhelmed during the night?
[That's completely okay. We have a quiet space available and friendly staff who understand. There's no pressure to stay in the main room the whole time — come and go as you need.]
What's the food like? Can you cater for dietary needs?
[We serve a full sit-down dinner with options for most dietary requirements including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and common allergies. Just note your needs when you book and we'll make sure you're sorted.]
Where do you run events?
[Currently in [your city/region — add here]. We choose accessible venues with good parking, public transport access, and wheelchair-friendly facilities. The address is confirmed when you book.]
How often do you run dinners?
[Once a month — same vibe, fresh night. We announce the next date and venue a few weeks in advance. Sign up to the newsletter below to be first to know.]
Can I volunteer or get involved?
[Yes — we love that you asked. Drop us a message using the form above and select "Something else". Tell us a bit about yourself and what you'd like to contribute. We'll get back to you.]
I'm a support coordinator. How does invoicing work?
[We're NDIS-friendly and can issue invoices to your plan manager or support coordinator. Head to our NDIS page for full details, or get in touch and we'll walk you through it.]
