Who We Are

A World That Celebrates Every Story

Feel Good Society runs monthly social events for people living with disabilities and mental health challenges, and for anyone who just wants to meet good people and feel good.

  • Celebration — We honour the strength and spirit of every person
  • Connection — We unite communities through shared humanity
  • Empowerment — We build confidence and opportunity for all

Our Values

Built on Three Foundations

Everything we do flows back to these three commitments.

🎉

Inclusion

Everyone is welcome, full stop. You don't need to explain yourself or tick any boxes. You just show up and you're in.

💜

Respect

We value each person's story and individuality. Your life experience is a strength, full stop. We see it, we celebrate it.

🌟

Belonging

We come together as one community where everyone feels at home. Not just tolerated. Genuinely, warmly, loudly welcomed.
