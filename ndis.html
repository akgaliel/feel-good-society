How It Works

NDIS Funding Made Simple

We know navigating the NDIS can feel complicated. That's why we've made it as straightforward as possible for participants to use their funding for Feel Good Society events.

Our events fall under the Social & Community Participation support category — one of the most flexible parts of an NDIS plan. If you have this funding, there's a good chance you can book with us right now.

  • ✓ No complicated paperwork — we handle the invoicing
  • ✓ Self-managed, plan-managed, and agency-managed all welcome
  • ✓ We work with your support coordinator if needed
  • ✓ Support workers are welcome to attend with you

What funding supports this?

FGS events can be claimed under the following NDIS support categories:

🤝

Social & Community Participation

The most common funding type for our events. Covers activities that build social skills and community connection.

🌟

Improved Daily Living

May also apply if your goals include building confidence and independent social participation.

💬

Not sure? Just ask us.

Message us and we'll work through your plan with you. No jargon, no judgment.

Step by Step

Booking with NDIS is Easy

Three simple steps and you're on the guest list.

1

Check Your Plan

Log into the myNDIS portal or call your plan manager. Look for Social & Community Participation or Improved Daily Living funding. Any amount there? You're likely good to go.

2

Get in Touch

Message us via the contact page or send us an email. We'll confirm eligibility, answer any questions, and provide a quote or service agreement if your plan manager needs one.

3

Come Along & Enjoy

Once we've sorted the funding side, you book your spot and show up. We handle everything else. All you need to do is walk through the door and have a great time.

Payment Options

We've Got Every Option Covered

NDIS or not, there's a way to attend that works for you.

NDIS Funding

Self-managed, plan-managed, or agency-managed — we work with all NDIS plan types. We can provide invoices, service agreements, or anything your plan manager needs.

Credit or Debit Card

Pay by card, bank transfer, or cash. If you're not on an NDIS plan or prefer to pay directly, just book through our booking page and pay at checkout. We accept Mastercard, Visa, Google Pay, and Apple Pay.

Afterpay

Split your ticket into 4 fortnightly payments at no extra cost. Select Afterpay at checkout on our booking page — it only takes a minute to set up if you're new to it.

Common Questions

NDIS FAQ

Can I use my NDIS plan to pay for a Feel Good Society event?
Yes, in most cases. If your NDIS plan includes Social & Community Participation or Improved Daily Living funding, our events are eligible. We recommend checking with your support coordinator or plan manager if you're unsure.
What NDIS management types do you support?
We work with self-managed, plan-managed, and agency-managed participants. Get in touch and let us know how your plan is managed — we'll provide the right paperwork, invoices, or service agreement to suit your situation.
Can my support worker come with me?
Absolutely. Support workers are welcome at every event. Note that a separate ticket is required for your support worker. Their ticket cost may also be claimable under your NDIS plan — check with your plan manager.
Do you provide service agreements?
Yes. If your plan manager or support coordinator needs a service agreement before you can book, just reach out and we'll get one sorted for you quickly. We know the paperwork side can be a hurdle and we're happy to help.
I'm not on the NDIS. Can I still come?
100%. Our events are open to everyone — NDIS participants, people without a plan, and anyone who just wants a great night out in an inclusive space. You don't need any disability or NDIS connection to attend.
What if I need help figuring out my funding?
Just send us a message. We're real people and we're happy to talk it through with you. We've helped many participants work out whether their plan covers our events. No jargon, no judgment — just an honest conversation.
